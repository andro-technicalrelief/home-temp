How to Register a New Domain

Finding the right domain is crucial for your brand. Use our Domain Search tool to check availability.

  1. Search for your desired domain name.
  2. Add it to your cart and proceed to checkout.
  3. Complete the registration details (Name, Address, Email).
  4. Once payment is confirmed, registration is usually instant.

Transferring a Domain

Moving your domain to Technical Relief allows you to manage everything in one place. Most transfers take 5-7 days depending on the TLD.

  • Unlock the Domain: Ensure your domain is unlocked at your current registrar.
  • Get EPP Code: Request the Auth/EPP code from your registrar.
  • Initiate Transfer: Enter the domain and code on our Transfer Page.

Managing DNS Records (A, CNAME, MX)

DNS (Domain Name System) translates your domain name into an IP address. You can manage this from the Client Area.

  • A Record: Points your domain to a specific server IP (e.g., your VPS).
  • CNAME: Aliases one domain to another (e.g., www to your root domain).
  • MX Record: Directs your email to a specific mail provider.

Understanding WHOIS Privacy

By default, domain registration data is public. We offer WHOIS Privacy to hide your personal information from the public database, protecting you from spam and identity theft.
