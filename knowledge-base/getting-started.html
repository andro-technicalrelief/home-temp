Getting Started

Step-by-step guides to help you get your Technical Relief account and services up and running.

How to Create Your Account

Creating an account with Technical Relief is the first step towards enterprise-grade managed hosting. Our registration process is straightforward and secure.

  1. Visit the Registration Page.
  2. Enter your full name, email address, and a strong password.
  3. Verify your email address via the link sent to your inbox.
  4. Complete your profile in the client area to enable all services.
Note: We recommend enabling 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication) immediately after your first login for maximum security.

Navigating the Client Dashboard

The Technical Relief client dashboard is where you manage all your services, billing, and support requests from a single interface.

  • Services: View and manage your active web hosting, VPS, and development projects.
  • Domains: Register new domains or manage DNS records for existing ones.
  • Billing: View invoices, update payment methods, and manage subscriptions.
  • Support: Open support tickets and view our documentation.

Connecting Your Domain to Hosting

Once you have a service and a domain, you need to link them. We handle most of this for you, but here is how to check your configuration.

If your domain is registered with us, it will be automatically pointed to your new hosting service. If it's registered elsewhere, you will need to update your Nameservers:

ns1.technicalrelief.co.za
ns2.technicalrelief.co.za

Setting Up Your First Website

Whether you're using WordPress or a custom Node.js application, getting your site live is easy.

For WordPress installations, use the 1-click installer in your control panel. For custom applications, our engineering team will assist with the initial deployment as part of our fully managed service.
