Understanding Your Technical Relief Invoice

We believe in transparent billing. Your invoice contains a detailed breakdown of all recurring hosting services and once-off development milestones.

  • Due Date: Invoices are due 7 days after generation.
  • Currency: All billing is processed in ZAR (South African Rand).
  • Itemization: Every server resource and service is clearly listed.

Payment Methods

We use PayFast as our primary payment gateway, offering secure and versatile payment options.

  • Credit/Debit Card: Instant processing for immediate service activation.
  • Instant EFT: Secure bank transfer for your convenience.
  • Recurring Debit: Automated monthly billing available via PayFast.

Upgrading or Downgrading Your Plan

Your business needs change, and our infrastructure scales with you.

You can request a plan change via the Client Area at any time. For VPS resources, upgrades are usually instant and do not require data migration. Downgrades may require engineer assistance to ensure data integrity.

Cancellation and Refund Policy

We stand by the quality of our service with a 30-day money-back guarantee for hosting and VPS plans.

Cancellations for recurring services must be submitted via the Client Area 7 days prior to your next billing cycle. Project-based development refunds are handled according to the specific service agreement signed at project commencement.
