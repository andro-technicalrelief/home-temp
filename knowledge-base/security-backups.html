🛡️ Security & Backups

Our comprehensive approach to protecting your digital assets and ensuring data persistence.

On this page

Understanding SSL Certificates

SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) encrypts the connection between your visitors and your server. It is mandatory for trust and SEO.

We provide free Let's Encrypt SSL certificates with all hosting and VPS plans. These are automatically installed and renewed for you — one less thing for you to manage.

How Our Backup System Works

Data persistence is our top priority. We employ a multi-tier backup strategy to ensure your data is always recoverable.

  • Daily Snapshots: Full server images taken every 24 hours.
  • Off-site Replication: Backups are encrypted and stored in a separate geographic location.
  • One-Click Restore: In the event of an issue, we can roll back your service to any point in the last 14 days.

Understanding DDoS Protection

DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service) attacks can cripple websites. Our infrastructure is protected by enterprise-grade filtering.

We utilize real-time traffic analysis to identify and scrub malicious traffic at the edge, before it reaches your server, ensuring your site remains operational even under attack.

Proactive Security Measures

We don't just wait for issues; we actively prevent them.

Every managed service includes mandatory security hardening: firewall optimization, brute-force protection (fail2ban), regular malware scanning, and proactive patching of core OS and server software.
