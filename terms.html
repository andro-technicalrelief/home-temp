4. Service Level Agreement (SLA)

4.1 Uptime Guarantee

Technical Relief guarantees 99.9% network uptime for shared hosting and 99.95% for VPS hosting plans, measured monthly. This excludes scheduled maintenance windows, which will be communicated at least 48 hours in advance.

4.2 SLA Credits

If monthly uptime falls below the guaranteed percentage, clients may request service credits:

  • 99.0% – 99.9% uptime: 10% monthly credit
  • 95.0% – 99.0% uptime: 25% monthly credit
  • Below 95.0% uptime: 50% monthly credit

SLA credits are applied to future invoices and do not constitute a refund. Credits must be requested within 30 days of the affected period.

4.3 SLA Exclusions

The SLA does not cover: (a) scheduled maintenance; (b) force majeure events; (c) client-caused issues (e.g., application crashes, exceeding resource limits); (d) third-party service outages beyond Technical Relief's control; (e) DNS propagation delays.
