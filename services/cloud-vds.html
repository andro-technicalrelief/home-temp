Performance of Dedicated

Cloud VDS (Virtual Dedicated Server) provides 100% guaranteed resources with hardware-level isolation. No neighbors, no contention.

🏗️

KVM Isolation

True hardware virtualization. Each VDS operates as a fully independent machine with its own kernel.

🚀

NVMe Array

Enterprise NVMe SSDs in RAID 10. Delivering hundreds of thousands of IOPS for data-intensive apps.

🌐

10Gbps Uplink

High-capacity networking with redundant carriers for low latency and high reliability.

Zero Contention

Isolated Resources

Unlike standard VPS where CPU cycles are shared, Cloud VDS pins your resources to specific hardware threads. Your performance is always consistent.
