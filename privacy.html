Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 1 February 2026 · Last Updated: 20 February 2026

Technical Relief ("Company", "we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Protection of Personal Information Act, 2013 (POPIA) and other applicable data protection legislation. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information Officer

In terms of POPIA, Technical Relief has appointed an Information Officer responsible for ensuring compliance with data protection requirements. For any privacy-related queries or requests, contact:

  • Information Officer: Technical Relief Privacy Team
  • Email: privacy@technicalrelief.co.za

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or purchase services, we collect:

  • Full name and surname
  • Email address
  • Physical and/or postal address
  • Contact phone number
  • Company name and registration number (if applicable)
  • VAT number (if applicable)
  • South African ID number (for .co.za domain registration, as required by the registry)

2.2 Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by PayFast (Pty) Ltd, a PCI DSS-compliant payment gateway. Technical Relief does not store, process, or have access to your full credit card numbers, bank account details, or payment card security codes. PayFast's privacy policy governs the processing of your payment data.

2.3 Technical Information

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referrer URL
  • Device identifiers

2.4 Service Data

When you use our hosting and VPS services, we process:

  • Server access logs (IP addresses, request timestamps)
  • Resource utilization data (CPU, memory, storage, bandwidth)
  • Email delivery logs (sender/recipient addresses, timestamps — not email content)
  • DNS query logs
  • Backup metadata

3. Purpose of Processing

We process your personal information for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery: To provision, manage, and maintain hosting, VPS, domain, and development services
  • Account management: To create and manage your client account, authenticate access, and process orders
  • Billing: To generate invoices, process payments via PayFast, and manage subscriptions
  • Communication: To send service notifications, maintenance alerts, security advisories, and support responses
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and respond to security threats, fraud, and abuse
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Service improvement: To analyse usage patterns and improve our services, infrastructure, and support

4. Legal Basis for Processing (POPIA)

We process your personal information on the following legal grounds under POPIA:

  • Consent: You provide consent when creating an account and accepting these terms
  • Contract: Processing necessary for the performance of our service agreements
  • Legal obligation: Processing required by law (e.g., tax records, domain registry requirements)
  • Legitimate interest: Processing necessary for security monitoring, fraud prevention, and service improvement

5. Cookies & Tracking

5.1 Cookies We Use

  • Essential cookies: Required for website functionality and client area authentication
  • Analytics cookies: To understand website usage and improve our services (anonymised data)
  • Preference cookies: To remember your settings and preferences

5.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may impair website functionality. We do not use cookies for targeted advertising.

6. Third-Party Data Sharing

We share personal information with the following categories of third parties, only to the extent necessary for service delivery:

  • PayFast (Pty) Ltd: Payment processing — receives name, email, and payment details for transaction processing
  • Billing platform (Blesta): Client account management, invoicing, and service provisioning automation
  • Infrastructure partners: Server provisioning and management — receives only technical data necessary for service delivery
  • Domain registries: Domain registration requires submission of registrant contact information as mandated by ICANN and local registries (e.g., ZACR for .co.za)
  • Legal authorities: When compelled by court order, subpoena, or other legal process

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes.

7. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your account plus 12 months after closure
  • Billing records: Retained for a minimum of 5 years as required by South African tax law (Income Tax Act, VAT Act)
  • Server logs: Retained for 90 days, then automatically purged
  • Backup data: Retained according to your plan's backup retention schedule, then permanently deleted
  • Support communications: Retained for 24 months after ticket closure

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest for sensitive data
  • Access controls and role-based permissions
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Intrusion detection and prevention systems
  • Physical security at data centre facilities
  • Employee security awareness training
  • Incident response procedures

9. Your Rights Under POPIA

As a data subject, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request confirmation of what personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Object: Object to the processing of your personal information on reasonable grounds
  • Withdraw consent: Withdraw previously given consent (this does not affect lawfulness of prior processing)
  • Data portability: Request your personal information in a structured, commonly used format
  • Complain: Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Information Officer at privacy@technicalrelief.co.za. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Cross-Border Data Transfers

In some cases, your data may be processed on servers located outside South Africa. Where this occurs, we ensure that the receiving country provides an adequate level of protection or that appropriate safeguards are in place, as required by Section 72 of POPIA.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will delete it promptly.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email to registered clients and updated on this page with a new "Last Updated" date. Continued use of our services after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

13. Information Regulator

If you are not satisfied with how we handle your data, you may lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator of South Africa:

  • Website: inforegulator.org.za
  • Email: complaints.IR@justice.gov.za

14. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@technicalrelief.co.za
  • Subject line: "Privacy Request — [Your Name]"
